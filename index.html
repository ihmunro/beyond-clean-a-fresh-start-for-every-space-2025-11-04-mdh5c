Our Story

About Fresh Feel Painting & Cleaning

Fresh Feel Painting & Cleaning was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to transform spaces and improve lives through exceptional painting and cleaning services. What started as a small, family-owned operation has grown into a trusted name in the industry, serving residential and commercial clients throughout the region. Our founders believed that quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and genuine customer care should be the foundation of every project. Over the years, Fresh Feel has maintained those core values while continuously innovating and expanding our capabilities to meet evolving customer needs.

Today, Fresh Feel's mission remains unwavering: to deliver outstanding results that exceed expectations while building lasting relationships with our clients. We believe in the transformative power of a fresh, clean space and the positive impact it has on well-being and productivity. Our core values of integrity, professionalism, reliability, and environmental responsibility guide every decision we make. We're committed to sustainable practices, continuous employee training, and maintaining the highest industry standards. Every project Fresh Feel undertakes reflects our dedication to excellence and our genuine commitment to making your space the best it can be.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Fresh Feel Painting and Cleaning services.

Fresh Feel provides comprehensive painting services for both residential and commercial properties. Our offerings include interior painting (walls, ceilings, trim), exterior painting (siding, fences, decks), cabinet refinishing, specialty finishes, and accent walls. We work with premium paint brands and can help you select the perfect colors and finishes for your space. Whether it's a small touch-up or a complete property repaint, our experienced team handles projects of all sizes with professional expertise and attention to detail.

Project duration varies depending on the scope, size, and complexity of the work. A single room interior painting typically takes 2-3 days, while whole-house projects may take 1-2 weeks. Exterior painting depends on weather conditions and surface preparation requirements. During our initial consultation, Fresh Feel provides detailed timelines based on your specific project. We pride ourselves on working efficiently without sacrificing quality, and we always keep you informed of our progress and any adjustments to the schedule.

There's no obligation, and we're happy to answer any questions you may have about Fresh Feel's services, pricing, and process.

Fresh Feel's comprehensive cleaning services include deep cleaning, regular maintenance cleaning, post-construction cleanup, move-in/move-out cleaning, and specialized services like window washing and floor care. We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for your family and the environment. Our team is trained in the latest cleaning techniques and pays meticulous attention to detail. Whether you need a one-time deep clean or ongoing maintenance, Fresh Feel customizes our services to meet your specific needs and schedule.
